# Garden Stage Concept

Stage: Garden / Moko  
Faction: The Green Claim  
Reference image: `stage_key_art.png`

## Identity

The Garden is clean but overclaimed territory. Moko's faction has turned stones, roots, flowers, mud paths, logs, and toy scraps into survival routes through a controlled green environment.

The stage should not feel wild because it is neglected. It should feel wild because Moko's faction intentionally adapted it into terrain that rewards endurance, observation, and respect for limits.

## Lore Purpose

The Green Claim rejects nostalgia. To Moko, the house is no longer a home; it is territory. This stage proves that toys survive by reading terrain, adapting, and abandoning fragile rituals.

The stage teaches the cost of survival logic: it creates resilience, but it can make softness and ceremony look useless.

## Visual Read

- Pristine garden elements arranged into survival paths.
- Toy-scale route language: stones, roots, hose crossings, log edges, flower canopy turns.
- Faction modifications: survival markers made from toy scraps, hidden green shortcuts, practical hazard signs.
- Mood: alert, grounded, natural, watchful, unsentimental.

## Route Fantasy

The race should feel adaptive and terrain-driven. Drivers cross stone bridges, cut under root gates, slide past mud patches, dodge log hazards, and find hidden green routes through flowers and leaves.

Shortcuts should feel earned by reading terrain, not by spectacle.

## Hero Landmarks

- Root gate over the main route.
- Stone bridge crossing.
- Hose crossing or water-slick line.
- Flower canopy tunnel.
- Survival marker cluster built from toy scraps.

## Hazards And Beats

- Root barriers: low overhead and edge hazards.
- Mud/leaf zones: surface change and traction risk.
- Log obstacles: static barriers or bumpable hazards.
- Hose crossing: slick, raised, or jumpable feature.
- Shortcut: hidden green route through flower canopy.

## Materials And Mood

- Materials: clean dirt, mud, leaf, stone, water, molded plastic scraps.
- Lighting: clear daylight-like garden light, soft foliage shadows.
- Palette: deep greens, earth browns, stone gray, Moko jungle accents.
- Audio mood: leafy percussion, mud slides, stone hits, water splashes, branch knocks.

- "Toy racing stone bridge and hose crossing set, clean garden terrain, playful but survival-focused, low-poly game asset, no logos, no text."
- "Flower canopy shortcut landmark for toy kart racing, oversized clean flowers and leaves, hidden route opening, readable from race camera."

## Blender Tasks

- Separate root gate, stone bridge, hose crossing, flower canopy, and marker props.
- Keep foliage broad and readable; avoid dense alpha-heavy leaves.
- Make collision helpers simple for roots, logs, and bridges.
- Create LOD-friendly versions of flower clusters.
- Ensure green shortcuts have clear openings from race camera.

## Godot Build Notes

- Author mud/leaf surface effects separately from mesh material.
- Use simple collision for logs and roots to prevent snagging.
- Keep hidden routes readable through lighting, shape, or color accents.
- Avoid foliage blocking camera at racing speed.
- Check mobile renderer cost for transparent foliage and water effects.
