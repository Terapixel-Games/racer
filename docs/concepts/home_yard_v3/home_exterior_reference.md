# Home Exterior Reference

## Design Intent

The exterior shell must read as a believable closed home, not a diagram of rooms. The roof, walls, windows, doors, trim, porch/deck transitions, gutters, vents, meters, foundation, and service side all belong to one coordinated envelope.

## Shape Language

Use one primary home mass with subordinate porch/deck/service elements. Roof planes need gable logic and attic agreement. Openings need frames, depth returns, sills, headers, and transparent glass. Exterior corners need trim or believable seams.

## Material Palette

Painted siding, roof shingles, fascia/soffit, gutters, trim boards, transparent glass, curtains or interior backdrops, foundation concrete, porch wood, exterior lights, service metal details.

## Gameplay Readability Notes

Exterior shell must not leak sky or voids through windows, roof seams, attic edges, or door headers. Close-up cameras should validate roofline, gables, transparent windows, porch threshold, service side, and foundation contact.

## Asset Prompt Notes

Meshy assets for windows, doors, porch/deck details, gutters, vents, meters, and trim should be modular low-poly pieces with real depth, readable glass, no text, no logos, and separate collision policy where needed.
