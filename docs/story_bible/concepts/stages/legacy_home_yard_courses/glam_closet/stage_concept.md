# Glam Closet Stage Concept

Stage: Glam Closet / Velva  
Faction: The Mirror Court  
Reference image: `stage_key_art.png`

## Identity

The Glam Closet is a dazzling display court. Velva's faction believes toys matter when they are seen, arranged, polished, and given status. Mirrors, vanities, perfume mist, jewelry boxes, glossy shelves, and display pedestals become a theatrical race route.

The stage should be beautiful and controlled, with the unsettling feeling that every toy has been assigned a role.

## Lore Purpose

The Mirror Court turns attention into survival. It rescues forgotten toys by making them visible, but it also ranks them by shine, usefulness, and audience.

The stage teaches that presentation can restore meaning, while also turning identity into performance.

## Visual Read

- Pristine closet surfaces: mirrors, shelves, boxes, fabric, gloss, glitter.
- Toy-scale route language: runway straights, mirror arch turns, pedestal ramps, vanity edges.
- Faction modifications: display ranks, court staging, polished gates, beauty-as-power symbols.
- Mood: glamorous, sharp, theatrical, seductive, controlling.

## Route Fantasy

The race should feel glossy and performative. Drivers sweep through mirror turns, sprint down vanity runways, cut through perfume mist, jump from jewelry-box ramps, and skim display pedestal edges.

Shortcuts should feel like status gates: beautiful, narrow, and risky, as if only the worthy are allowed through.

## Hero Landmarks

- Mirror arch framing a major turn.
- Vanity runway straight.
- Perfume cloud zone.
- Jewelry-box ramp.
- Display pedestal turn sequence.

## Hazards And Beats

- Perfume mist: visual obstruction or slow/confusion hazard.
- Mirror turn: reflective landmark and tight corner.
- Jewelry clutter: bumpable hazards.
- Pedestal edges: elevated narrow line.
- Shortcut: status gate through mirror arch or vanity drawer.

## Materials And Mood

- Materials: mirror gloss, glitter plastic, polished fabric, shiny metal, cosmetic powder color.
- Lighting: bright vanity lights, clean highlights, controlled sparkle.
- Palette: pink, pearl, mirror silver, velvet darks, Velva glam accents.
- Audio mood: sparkle whooshes, perfume puffs, glossy UI hits, runway pulse.

## Blender Tasks

- Split mirror arch, vanity runway, jewelry-box ramp, pedestal set, and perfume bottle props.
- Keep mirror surfaces simple and performance-friendly.
- Prepare collision helpers for elevated pedestal paths.
- Remove tiny cosmetic details that add noise from race camera.
- Use material slots for mirror, glitter plastic, fabric, metal, accent.

## Godot Build Notes

- Use shader/particle effects for perfume mist; keep gameplay collision separate.
- Avoid real mirror rendering unless budget allows; fake reflections with materials.
- Keep glossy surfaces readable and not visually noisy at speed.
- Make elevated pedestal paths wide enough for player control.
- Ensure shortcut gates are visible before commitment.
