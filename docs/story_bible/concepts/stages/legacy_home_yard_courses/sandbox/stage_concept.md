# Sandbox Stage Concept

Stage: Sandbox / Rexx  
Faction: The Sand Throne  
Reference image: `stage_key_art.png`

## Identity

The Sandbox is conquered territory ruled through strength. Rexx has turned sand, buckets, bones, plastic tracks, shovels, and tribute piles into a heavy, aggressive stage that makes every racer feel exposed.

The space should feel clean and arranged, not dirty. It is a pristine play sandbox converted into a throne arena by a toy who believes power is the only protection that matters.

## Lore Purpose

The Sand Throne proves Rexx's argument that waiting is weakness. This room is where scared toys were taken, recruited, or claimed after the Seventh Night.

The stage teaches that Rexx does protect his territory, but protection comes with tribute, obedience, and fear.

## Visual Read

- Clean sand play area with crisp toy-scale berms and sculpted lanes.
- Dominant silhouette: ridge throne, bucket tunnel, fossil arch, shovel ramp.
- Faction modifications: tribute piles, claimed banners, intimidation markers, heavy track supports.
- Mood: exposed, hot, dominant, forceful, territorial.

## Route Fantasy

The race should feel heavy and aggressive. Drivers climb sand berms, punch through bucket tunnels, jump fossil arches, cut over shovel ramps, and pass below Rexx's throne ridge.

Shortcuts should feel like proving strength: faster but rougher lines with less guard rail and more recovery risk.

## Hero Landmarks

- Sand ridge throne overlooking the route.
- Overturned bucket tunnel.
- Fossil arch jump.
- Shovel ramp into a hard landing.
- Tribute pile of claimed toys, parts, flags, and plastic scraps.

## Hazards And Beats

- Sand berms: uneven route edges and jump lips.
- Fossil ribs: visual gates and collision hazards.
- Bucket tunnel: compressed visibility and strong silhouette.
- Shovel ramp: high-speed stunt beat.
- Shortcut: unstable ridge line along the throne slope.

## Materials And Mood

- Materials: clean sand, bright plastic bucket, fossil stone, toy track plastic, matte shovel plastic.
- Lighting: bright, clean, high contrast.
- Palette: sand gold, Rexx red/orange, bone off-white, bucket blue/yellow accents.
- Audio mood: gritty slide, plastic scoop hits, fossil clacks, dino-stomp stingers.

## Blender Tasks

- Split sandbox landmarks into throne ridge, bucket tunnel, fossil arch, shovel ramp, tribute props.
- Keep sand shapes low-poly and collision-friendly.
- Use separate invisible collision for berms and ramps.
- Avoid tiny tribute details that disappear from race camera.
- Make all tunnels large enough for kart and camera clearance.

## Godot Build Notes

- Author rough/slow sand zones separately from visual sand meshes.
- Keep bucket tunnel entry visible from long approach.
- Use fossil arch as a checkpoint framing device if useful.
- Ensure high jumps have clean reset rules and no camera clipping.
- Keep tribute pile outside primary collision path unless designed as hazard.
