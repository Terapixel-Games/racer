# Bedroom Stage Concept

Stage: Bedroom / Tuggs  
Faction: The Soft Room  
Reference image: `stage_key_art.png`

## Identity

The Bedroom is a protected waiting refuge. Tuggs' faction keeps toys clean, arranged, and ready because they believe the family will return. The room is soft, warm, pristine, and emotionally tense.

This stage should look safe at first, but the safety is restrictive. Blankets, beds, rugs, lamps, and toy blocks become careful lanes, guarded shelters, and soft barriers that keep racers inside Tuggs' idea of protection.

## Lore Purpose

The Soft Room exists to preserve every toy until the family comes back. It shelters the frightened and damaged, but it also treats leaving, changing, or giving up hope as a threat.

The stage teaches Tuggs' central contradiction: no toy should be discarded, but a refuge can become a cage.

## Visual Read

- Pristine bedroom: made bed, clean rug, dust-free furniture, warm bedside light.
- Toy-scale route language: soft ramps, blanket tunnels, block borders, plush safety rails.
- Faction modifications: triage corner, waiting line of preserved toys, clean tags, gentle but firm barriers.
- Mood: warm, loyal, mournful, protective, claustrophobic.

## Route Fantasy

The race should feel soft but restrictive. Drivers weave through bed ramps, slide into blanket tunnels, cross rugs with grip changes, pass the bedside-lamp beacon, and squeeze through guarded safe zones.

The best shortcuts should feel like slipping out of protection: faster lines through folded blankets or under furniture that Tuggs' faction would rather keep closed.

## Hero Landmarks

- Bed ramp with pillow guard rails.
- Blanket tunnel that compresses visibility.
- Bedside-lamp beacon acting as the emotional lighthouse of the room.
- Toy triage corner with repaired, wrapped, or carefully arranged toys.
- Waiting line of preserved toys facing the bedroom door.

## Hazards And Beats

- Blanket folds: soft walls and partial occluders.
- Rug grip zones: slower but stable surface.
- Toy block barriers: bumpable or breakaway lane dividers.
- Lamp-light safe zone: clear checkpoint framed by warmth.
- Shortcut: low route under bed or through blanket fold, faster but narrow.

## Materials And Mood

- Materials: plush fabric, carpet, warm plastic, painted wood, soft cloth.
- Lighting: warm lamp pools with clean shadows, no dust.
- Palette: warm creams, soft browns, muted toy colors, Tuggs comfort accents.
- Audio mood: muffled room tone, plush bumps, blanket slides, soft toy impacts.

## Blender Tasks

- Convert fabric landmarks into low-poly readable silhouettes rather than high-detail cloth noise.
- Separate blanket tunnel, pillow rails, bed ramp, and toy-block props.
- Create collision helpers with generous clearances for tunnels.
- Keep soft materials visually distinct from hard toy blocks.
- Preserve toy-scale proportions so the room reads large but driveable.

## Godot Build Notes

- Author blanket tunnels with simple collision and clear camera clearance.
- Mark rug areas as optional grip/slow zones if gameplay supports surface metadata.
- Use the waiting line as visual dressing only, outside racing collision.
- Keep soft barriers readable at speed with strong color/edge contrast.
- Avoid dark occlusion inside tunnels; the route must stay playable.
